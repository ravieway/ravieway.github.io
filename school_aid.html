School Aid Web App

The objective of this project was to develop an efficient web-based system to find and track donor assistance for supporting needy schools and students. The system includes features to input school details and identify underdeveloped sectors in need of assistance. Donors who are willing to support can register in the system, providing their details and entering the monetary contribution they are fully or partially willing to offer for specific projects.
